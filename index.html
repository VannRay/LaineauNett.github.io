Home & Textile Ecology · Consulting · Workshops

A practice rooted in the belief that health is shaped by our environment: the fibres we wear, the air in our homes, the materials we sleep in, and the substances we bring into daily life.

Practice

Health is not only what we put into the body, but what surrounds it. This work explores the relationship between materials, home environments, and everyday rituals — with attention to natural fibres, non-toxic systems, and Canadian-made sourcing.
